About MPA School

Welcome to ABC School! We are dedicated to providing high-quality education to our students. Metropolitan Academy's first Campus was founded in 1967 at Naseerabad by Mr. Muhammad Rizwan(late). The Academy aims at increasing the knowledge and developing cognitive and meta cognitive skills of students. Metropolitan Academy is a co educational institution provided with all the staple facilities such as laboratories, library, auditorium, air conditioned Montessori class rooms and computer laboratories. Metropolitan academy has branches at Naseerabad, Metropolitan Academy II at Incholi, Metropolitan School III at Gulberg, Metropolitan Foundation School IV at Azizabad, The Metropolitan Academy V at Gulistan e Jauhar, The Metropolitan Academy VI at Gulshan e Iqbal, Metropolitan School VII at North Karachi.
